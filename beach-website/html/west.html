Camps Bay Beach, South Africa

Camps Bay Beach, a Blue Flag beach in Cape Town, South Africa, is a popular tourist destination known for its white sand, turquoise waters, and the Twelve Apostles mountain range backdrop, offering a Mediterranean vibe with trendy eateries and nightlife. Known for its stunning views and vibrant atmosphere.

Santa Monica Beach, California

Santa Monica State Beach, a popular Southern California beach, is known for its iconic Santa Monica Pier, 3.5 miles of sandy shores, and various activities like swimming, surfing, and biking. Famous for its pier and beautiful sunsets.
