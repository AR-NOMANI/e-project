Miami Beach, Florida (USA)

Miami Beach is a vibrant island city in South Florida, connected to mainland Miami by bridges. Its wide beaches stretch from North Shore Open Space Park to South Pointe Park, with the famous South Beach known for its celebrity presence and Art Deco architecture. With its lively nightlife, white sands, and turquoise waters, Miami Beach is a premier tourist destination.
